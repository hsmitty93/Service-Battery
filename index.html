Welcome to Service Battery

Service Battery, Inc., sells and services industrial and deep cycle batteries. We are an authorized dealer for Crown industrial motor power batteries and MAC commercial and industrial grade chargers. New, used and reconditioned batteries are available for sale or rent. We provide diagnostics and evaluations to access the work performance of your battery. Service and repairs are provided on an as-needed basis at either your location or at our location. We offer maintenance programs which are are customized to your needs, providing regular service at your location. With a maintenance program, you can rely on Service Battery, Inc., to keep your batteries reliably working to their maximum potential.
